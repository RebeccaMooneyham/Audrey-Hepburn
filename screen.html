Audrey’s career included both movies, television, and the stage. The American Film Institute named Hepburn third among the Greatest Female Stars of All Time. She is one of few entertainers who have won Academy, Emmy, Grammy, and Tony Awards. She won a record three BAFTA Awards for Best British Actress in a Leading World. She received a tribute from the Film Society of Lincoln Center and was frequent presenter at the Academy Awards. Below is a list of her acting credits.
